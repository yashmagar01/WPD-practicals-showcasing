Indeed, its pleasure to introduce you with Government Polytechnic, Awasari (Khurd) in rural Pune district. Choosing the right institute for your ward and branch is one of the important decision, you will make in your life. I am delighted as you have considered Government Polytechnic, Awasari. Government Polytechnic, Awasari is established in 2008, with 4-branches (IT, Computer, Electronics and Telecommunication, Automobile) and in 2009 (Civil, Electrical and Mechanical) having land area of 17-acres surrounded by serene beauty of nature. College campus has very good infrastructure in terms of various departments, Girls Hostel and Boys Hostel, staff quarters, Gymkhana facilities.

Institute has 7-branches namely: Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics and Telecommunication, Automobile, Computer and information Technology with each of 60-student intake capacity and total 420-students. I am Happy to state you here that 5- branches: Information Technology, Mechanical Engineering, Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering, Electrical Engineering and Computer Engineering has been granted accreditation status. The success of accreditation is due to planned and disciplined environment of academics and co-curricular activities. The students and the faculties are of inquisitive attitude as per as the academic excellence is concerned.

The institute has an active placement cell. Renowned companies select the students through campus drive. We inculcate ethical values amongst students. We, at Government Polytechnic Awasari are focused on Education & Training, Teaching & Learning, Projects & Outreach through the Industry – Institution Interactions. As a head of the institute, I take this opportunity to extend my Best wishes to all students to achieve success at campus and future career endeavors.
